Version 1 - Version 2 - Version 3

Version 3 is a concept where you can see all reports for the current day and whether they have been reported or not. If they are not reported you can press "Notify" and it would send a notification to the player. Once you click the button it will change to "Notified" so you can keep track on who you have notified and not, but you can keep pressing the button to send more if they keep ignoring it.
